# Spiritual Gifts Assessment Questions - DRAFT v2

**Status:** DRAFT v2 - Awaiting theological review
**Total Questions:** 96 (updated from 90)
**Format:** Mix of Likert scale (1-5) and behavioral scenarios
**Presentation:** Questions will be randomized within each tier

---

## Tier 1: Serving Gifts (Romans 12 + others)

**44 questions total across 11 gift categories (4 questions each)**

**IMPORTANT:** These questions will be presented in RANDOM order to users, not grouped by gift category.

### Gift Categories:
1. Serving (practical helps) - 4 questions
2. Teaching - 4 questions
3. Encouraging - 4 questions
4. Giving - 4 questions
5. Administration - 4 questions
6. Mercy - 4 questions
7. Hospitality - 4 questions
8. Evangelism - 4 questions
9. Worship/Music - 4 questions
10. Intercessory Prayer - 4 questions
11. Craftsmanship/Arts - 4 questions

---

### SERVING (4 questions)

**Q1 (Likert):** I notice practical needs before others do.
- 1 = Strongly Disagree
- 2 = Disagree
- 3 = Neutral
- 4 = Agree
- 5 = Strongly Agree

**Q2 (Likert):** I find joy in meeting tangible needs (setting up, cleaning, fixing things, etc.).

**Q3 (Behavioral):** At a group gathering, I am most likely to:
- a) Help set up chairs, serve food, or clean up
- b) Lead the discussion or teach
- c) Encourage and pray for attendees
- d) Make sure everyone feels welcomed and included

**Q4 (Likert):** I feel most fulfilled when I'm serving others behind the scenes.

---

### TEACHING (4 questions)

**Q5 (Likert):** I enjoy breaking down complex biblical concepts into simple, understandable terms.

**Q6 (Likert):** I love studying Scripture and helping others understand what it means.

**Q7 (Behavioral):** When someone asks me a theological question, I:
- a) Explain it thoroughly using Scripture and examples
- b) Share my personal experience with that truth
- c) Encourage them to search the Scriptures themselves
- d) Direct them to a resource or someone more knowledgeable

**Q8 (Likert):** I get energized when I have the opportunity to explain biblical truths to others.

---

### ENCOURAGING (4 questions)

**Q9 (Likert):** I am naturally drawn to speak life, hope, and strength into others.

**Q10 (Likert):** I can easily see the best in people and help them see it too.

**Q11 (Behavioral):** When a friend shares a struggle with me, I:
- a) Speak words of encouragement and remind them of God's truth
- b) Listen deeply and provide comfort
- c) Help them find a practical solution
- d) Pray with them for breakthrough

**Q12 (Likert):** I often find myself sending encouraging texts or messages to people.

---

### GIVING (4 questions)

**Q13 (Likert):** I am generous with my resources (time, money, possessions, skills).

**Q14 (Likert):** I find joy in meeting financial or material needs for others.

**Q15 (Behavioral):** When I hear about someone in financial need, I:
- a) Immediately look for ways to give sacrificially
- b) Pray about how God wants me to help
- c) Encourage them and offer emotional support
- d) Trust that God will provide for them

**Q16 (Likert):** I regularly look for opportunities to be generous, even when it costs me something.

---

### ADMINISTRATION (4 questions)

**Q17 (Likert):** I naturally take initiative to organize people and resources toward a goal.

**Q18 (Likert):** I can see the big picture and create a plan to accomplish it.

**Q19 (Behavioral):** When a project needs to be completed, I:
- a) Step up to organize people, delegate tasks, and ensure it gets done
- b) Offer to help with specific tasks someone assigns me
- c) Pray for wisdom and clarity for those leading
- d) Encourage the team and keep morale high

**Q20 (Likert):** I am energized when I'm given responsibility to lead or organize.

---

### MERCY (4 questions)

**Q21 (Likert):** I feel others' pain deeply and am moved to bring comfort.

**Q22 (Likert):** I am naturally drawn to people who are hurting, suffering, or marginalized.

**Q23 (Behavioral):** When I encounter someone who is grieving or in pain, I:
- a) Sit with them, listen deeply, and offer compassion without trying to fix it
- b) Pray for them and ask God to heal their pain
- c) Look for practical ways to help ease their burden
- d) Encourage them with Scripture and remind them of hope

**Q24 (Likert):** I find it easy to show compassion and patience to people others might overlook.

---

### HOSPITALITY (4 questions)

**Q25 (Likert):** I love opening my home to others and making them feel welcome.

**Q26 (Likert):** I am naturally good at creating environments where people feel comfortable and included.

**Q27 (Behavioral):** When I have guests in my home, I:
- a) Focus on making sure they feel welcomed, comfortable, and cared for
- b) Use the opportunity to teach or share spiritual insights
- c) Serve their practical needs (food, drinks, comfort)
- d) Pray with them and minister to their spiritual needs

**Q28 (Likert):** I find joy in hosting gatherings, meals, or events where people can connect.

---

### EVANGELISM (4 questions)

**Q29 (Likert):** I am passionate about sharing the gospel with people who don't know Jesus.

**Q30 (Likert):** I naturally look for opportunities to tell others about my faith.

**Q31 (Behavioral):** When I meet someone who doesn't know Christ, I:
- a) Look for opportunities to share the gospel with them
- b) Build relationship first and wait for the right time
- c) Pray for them and trust God will send someone
- d) Invite them to church or a Christian event

**Q32 (Likert):** I feel a burden for people who are far from God and want to reach them.

---

### WORSHIP/MUSIC (4 questions)

**Q33 (Likert):** I feel most connected to God when I'm worshiping Him through music.

**Q34 (Likert):** I have a natural ability to lead others into God's presence through worship.

**Q35 (Behavioral):** When I'm in a worship setting, I:
- a) Feel called to use music to help others encounter God
- b) Worship personally and focus on my own connection with God
- c) Serve in practical ways to support the worship experience
- d) Pray for others to experience God's presence

**Q36 (Likert):** I use (or want to use) music as a way to minister to others and glorify God.

---

### INTERCESSORY PRAYER (4 questions)

**Q37 (Likert):** I feel called to spend extended time praying for others.

**Q38 (Likert):** I often sense when someone needs prayer, even if they haven't told me.

**Q39 (Behavioral):** When I hear about a need or crisis, I:
- a) Immediately begin praying and feel burdened to intercede
- b) Offer to help practically
- c) Encourage the person with words of hope
- d) Trust that God is in control

**Q40 (Likert):** I find great joy and fulfillment in standing in the gap for others through prayer.

---

### CRAFTSMANSHIP/ARTS (4 questions)

**Q41 (Likert):** I use creative skills (art, design, building, crafts) to serve God and others.

**Q42 (Likert):** I believe God has given me talents in creating things that bring Him glory.

**Q43 (Behavioral):** When I see a need for something creative (design, building, art), I:
- a) Feel inspired to create something beautiful or functional for God's glory
- b) Appreciate the creativity but leave it to others
- c) Pray for those with creative gifts to step up
- d) Help in practical ways to support the creative process

**Q44 (Likert):** I am energized when I can use my hands or creative skills to serve the church or others.

## Tier 2: Supernatural Gifts (1 Corinthians 12:7-11)

**27 questions total across 9 gift categories (3 questions each)**
**Format:** Mix of DESIRE questions and EXPERIENCE questions

**IMPORTANT:** These questions will be presented in RANDOM order to users, not grouped by gift category.

### Gift Categories:
1. Word of Wisdom - 3 questions
2. Word of Knowledge - 3 questions
3. Faith - 3 questions
4. Gifts of Healing - 3 questions
5. Miracles - 3 questions
6. Prophecy (rhema) - 3 questions
7. Discerning of Spirits - 3 questions
8. Tongues - 3 questions
9. Interpretation of Tongues - 3 questions

---

### WORD OF WISDOM (3 questions)

**Q45 (Desire - Likert):** I want to receive supernatural wisdom from God to guide others in their decisions.

**Q46 (Experience - Likert):** I have spoken God's wisdom into a situation and seen it bring clarity or breakthrough.

**Q47 (Experience - Behavioral):** When someone asks me for advice about a major decision, I:
- a) Wait and ask God for His specific wisdom before responding
- b) Share biblical principles and let them decide
- c) Encourage them and pray with them
- d) Help them think through practical pros and cons

---

### WORD OF KNOWLEDGE (3 questions)

**Q48 (Desire - Likert):** I want God to reveal information about people or situations that I couldn't know naturally.

**Q49 (Experience - Likert):** I have received a word, picture, or insight about someone that I had no way of knowing naturally.

**Q50 (Experience - Behavioral):** During prayer for someone, I:
- a) Often receive pictures, words, or impressions about their life or situation
- b) Pray Scripture and biblical truths over them
- c) Ask them to share what they need prayer for
- d) Speak encouragement and hope over them

---

### FAITH (3 questions)

**Q51 (Desire - Likert):** I want to believe God for impossible situations and see Him move.

**Q52 (Experience - Likert):** I have prayed for something impossible and seen God do it.

**Q53 (Behavioral):** When faced with an impossible situation, I:
- a) Immediately believe God can and will do the impossible
- b) Pray and ask God for wisdom on how to respond
- c) Look for practical solutions
- d) Trust God's timing and sovereignty

---

### GIFTS OF HEALING (3 questions)

**Q54 (Desire - Likert):** I want to pray for the sick and see them healed.

**Q55 (Experience - Likert):** I have prayed for someone with physical pain or sickness and they experienced healing.

**Q56 (Behavioral):** When I hear that someone is sick or in pain, I:
- a) Immediately want to lay hands on them and pray for healing
- b) Offer to help them practically (bring meals, run errands, etc.)
- c) Encourage them with Scripture and pray for God's will
- d) Trust that God will heal them in His timing

---

### MIRACLES (3 questions)

**Q57 (Desire - Likert):** I want to see God perform miraculous signs and wonders through me.

**Q58 (Experience - Likert):** I have witnessed or been part of a miracle that defied natural explanation.

**Q59 (Likert):** I believe God wants to do the impossible through ordinary people like me.

---

### PROPHECY - RHEMA (3 questions)

**Q60 (Desire - Likert):** I want to hear God's "now word" for specific people and situations.

**Q61 (Experience - Likert):** I have spoken a prophetic word over someone that later proved to be accurate or timely.

**Q62 (Behavioral):** When I sense God showing me something about someone's future or calling, I:
- a) Share it with them directly and invite them to test it
- b) Pray about it and wait for confirmation before speaking
- c) Write it down and ask God when to share it
- d) Keep it to myself unless they ask

---

### DISCERNING OF SPIRITS (3 questions)

**Q63 (Desire - Likert):** I want to be able to discern what spirit is at work in a person or situation.

**Q64 (Experience - Likert):** I have sensed whether the Holy Spirit, human spirit, or demonic spirit was at work in a situation.

**Q65 (Behavioral):** When I'm in a room or conversation, I:
- a) Often sense the spiritual atmosphere (peace, oppression, God's presence, etc.)
- b) Focus on what people are saying and how they're feeling
- c) Look for practical ways to serve or help
- d) Pray silently and trust God is in control

---

### TONGUES (3 questions)

**Q66 (Desire - Likert):** I want to receive the gift of praying in tongues (unknown languages).

**Q67 (Experience - Likert):** I have prayed in tongues (a language I've never learned).

**Q68 (Likert):** I use (or want to use) tongues regularly in my personal prayer time.

---

### INTERPRETATION OF TONGUES (3 questions)

**Q69 (Desire - Likert):** I want to be able to interpret when someone prays in tongues.

**Q70 (Experience - Likert):** I have heard someone pray in tongues and received an interpretation of what was being prayed.

**Q71 (Behavioral):** When someone prays in tongues in a group setting, I:
- a) Ask God what He's saying and wait for an interpretation
- b) Pray along with them in agreement
- c) Listen quietly and trust God is at work
- d) Feel unsure about what's happening

## Tier 3: Leadership Calling (Ephesians 4:11)

**25 questions total across 5 gift categories (5 questions each)**
**Format:** CONFIRMATION (what others see) + CALLING (what you sense)

**IMPORTANT:** These questions will be presented in RANDOM order to users, not grouped by gift category.

### Gift Categories:
1. Apostle - 5 questions
2. Prophet - 5 questions
3. Evangelist - 5 questions
4. Shepherd/Pastor - 5 questions
5. Teacher - 5 questions

---

### APOSTLE (5 questions)

**Q72 (Confirmation - Likert):** Others have told me I have a gift for starting new things or building systems.

**Q73 (Calling - Likert):** I feel called to establish kingdom culture in new environments.

**Q74 (Likert):** I am energized by pioneering and building from scratch.

**Q75 (Behavioral):** When I see a broken system or structure, I:
- a) Envision how to rebuild it and mobilize others to create something new
- b) Pray for those affected and offer encouragement
- c) Help within the current structure as best I can
- d) Look for a better opportunity elsewhere

**Q76 (Confirmation - Likert):** People have said I'm good at gathering teams and building movements.

---

### PROPHET (5 questions)

**Q77 (Confirmation - Likert):** People have told me I see and hear from God in unique ways.

**Q78 (Calling - Likert):** I feel a burden to speak what God is saying, even if it's corrective.

**Q79 (Likert):** I often see things in the spiritual realm that others miss.

**Q80 (Behavioral):** When I sense God showing me something about the future, I:
- a) Share it with leadership for discernment and action
- b) Pray about it privately and wait for confirmation
- c) Write it down and ask God when to release it
- d) Trust that God will reveal it to those who need to know

**Q81 (Confirmation - Likert):** Others have recognized that I carry insight or revelation from God.

---

### EVANGELIST (5 questions)

**Q82 (Confirmation - Likert):** Others have noticed I'm naturally good at sharing my faith.

**Q83 (Calling - Likert):** I feel most alive when I'm reaching people who don't know Jesus.

**Q84 (Likert):** I am burdened for the lost and think about them often.

**Q85 (Behavioral):** When I meet someone who doesn't know Christ, I:
- a) Look for opportunities to share the gospel and invite them into relationship with Jesus
- b) Build relationship first and look for the right time to share
- c) Pray for them and trust God will send the right person
- d) Invite them to church or a Christian event

**Q86 (Confirmation - Likert):** People have told me I have a gift for helping others come to faith.

---

### SHEPHERD/PASTOR (5 questions)

**Q87 (Confirmation - Likert):** People naturally come to me when they're hurting.

**Q88 (Calling - Likert):** I feel called to care for and nurture God's people.

**Q89 (Likert):** I am energized by one-on-one time with people, not large crowds.

**Q90 (Behavioral):** When someone shares a deep struggle with me, I:
- a) Listen deeply, care for them, and walk with them through it over time
- b) Pray for breakthrough and speak truth over their situation
- c) Help them find practical solutions or resources
- d) Encourage them and point them to Scripture

**Q91 (Confirmation - Likert):** Others have said I create a safe space where people feel known and cared for.

---

### TEACHER (5 questions)

**Q92 (Confirmation - Likert):** Others have told me I explain Scripture clearly and accurately.

**Q93 (Calling - Likert):** I feel called to equip the saints with sound biblical doctrine.

**Q94 (Likert):** I love studying the Bible and helping others understand it deeply.

**Q95 (Behavioral):** When I read Scripture, I:
- a) Naturally think about how to teach it to others and make it clear
- b) Apply it personally to my own life first
- c) Look for prophetic insights or what God is saying now
- d) Pray it over people and situations

**Q96 (Confirmation - Likert):** People have said I have a gift for making complex biblical truths accessible.
