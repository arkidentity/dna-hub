## üó∫Ô∏è THE COMPLETE JOURNEY TIMELINE

### BEFORE DNA GROUP STARTS

#### **Complete DNA Discipleship Manual**
- 6 weekly sessions to prepare your heart
- Take the Personal Dam Assessment first (optional)
- Complete the DNA Readiness Quiz after Session 6

#### **Phase 0: Pre-Launch (6 weeks)**
Use: DNA Launch Guide Phase 0
- Week 1-2: Self-assessment & find co-leader
- Week 3-4: Prayer strategy & identify disciples
- Week 5-6: Invite disciples & set expectations
- Create DNA Group Agreement
- Schedule Week 1 meeting

---

### DNA GROUP BEGINS (12 MONTHS TOTAL)

#### **PHASE 1: Foundation (Months 1-3 / Weeks 1-12)**
Use: 90-Day Toolkit

**Month 1 - Foundation (Weeks 1-4)**
- Week 1: Life Assessment (baseline)
- Week 2: 3D Journal
- Week 3: 4D Prayer Rhythm
- Week 4: Creed Cards

**Month 2 - Deepening (Weeks 5-8)**
- Week 5: Q&A Deep Dive
- Week 6: Listening Prayer Circle
- Week 7: Outreach/Mission Activity
- Week 8: Testimony Time + Debrief

**Month 3 - Breakthrough (Weeks 9-12)**
- Week 9: Breaking Strongholds
- Week 10: Identity Activation Workshop
- Week 11: Spiritual Gifts Discovery
- Week 12: Life Assessment Revisited (measure growth)

---

#### **PHASE 2: Growth & Empowerment (Months 4-6)**
Use: DNA Launch Guide Phase 2

**What changes:**
- Disciples begin co-facilitating meetings (50% of content)
- Use Supplemental Lessons (standalone topics for continued growth)
- More coaching, less teaching from leaders
- Disciples practice leading discussions, prayer, and ministry

**Goal:** Build confidence and competence in facilitation

---

#### **PHASE 3: Multiplication Prep (Months 7-12)**
Use: DNA Launch Guide Phase 3

**What changes:**
- Disciples facilitate 75%+ of meetings
- Leaders primarily coach and prepare disciples to launch
- Begin planning for multiplication (who will lead their own group?)
- Use DNA Readiness Quiz to assess multiplication readiness

**Month 9-10:** Have "The Multiplication Conversation"
**Month 12:** DNA group multiplies into 2-4 new groups

---

### AFTER MULTIPLICATION

**You've successfully multiplied!** Now what?

**Option 1: Start Another DNA Group**
- Begin Phase 0 again with new disciples
- Your former disciples are now your peer network

**Option 2: Mentor Multiple DNA Leaders**
- Meet monthly with your multiplied leaders
- Coach them through challenges
- Celebrate their wins

**Option 3: Train DNA Leaders**
- Lead cohorts through the DNA Discipleship Manual
- Prepare multiple leaders to launch groups simultaneously

**The goal:** Exponential multiplication across your city

## ‚ùì FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

**Q: Do I have to complete the DNA Discipleship Manual first?**
A: Strongly recommended. It prepares your heart and theology. If you skip it, you may struggle with foundational concepts in the Launch Guide.

**Q: Can I use the 90-Day Toolkit for months 4-12?**
A: The 90-Day Toolkit is specifically designed for the first 12 weeks (Phase 1). After that, use the DNA Launch Guide for Phases 2-3, which gives you more flexibility to customize.

**Q: What if my disciples aren't ready to multiply after 12 months?**
A: Extend Phase 3 as needed. Some disciples need 18-24 months. Don't rush multiplication‚Äîit's about readiness, not timeline.

**Q: Can I skip Phase 0?**
A: No. Phase 0 is critical for setting up your group well. Rushing into Week 1 without proper preparation leads to preventable problems.

**Q: Do I need to use every tool in the 90-Day Toolkit?**
A: For the first 12 weeks, yes‚Äîfollow the Toolkit exactly. It's been designed to build sequentially. After Week 12, you have more freedom to customize.
