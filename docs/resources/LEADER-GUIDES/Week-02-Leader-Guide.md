### 1. Check-In + Opening Prayer (10 min)

### 2. Teaching: What is 3D Journal? (20 min)
**The 3 D's:**
1. **DISCOVERY** ‚Äì What does the text say? (Observe)
2. **DECLARATION** ‚Äì What is God saying to me? (Apply)
3. **DEVOTION** ‚Äì What will I do in response? (Obey)

**Why this works:**
- Moves from head knowledge ‚Üí heart transformation ‚Üí hands obedience
- Simple enough for anyone, deep enough for lifelong growth
- Takes 10-15 minutes daily

### 3. Live Demonstration (15 min)
**Leader demonstrates:**
- Pick a Scripture (e.g., Psalm 23:1)
- Model Discovery: "What do I observe? The Lord is my shepherd..."
- Model Declaration: "God, what are You saying to me through this?"
- Model Devotion: "I will trust You today with [specific situation]."

### 4. Practice Together (30 min)
- Give everyone the same Scripture
- 10 min silent journaling
- Share what God said (5 min each person)

### 5. Challenge + Closing (15 min)
**This week's challenge:**
- Do 3D Journal daily for 7 days
- Share one journal entry in group chat this week
