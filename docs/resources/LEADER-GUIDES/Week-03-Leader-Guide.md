### 2. Teaching: The 4D Prayer Rhythm (25 min)
**The 4 D's:**
1. **DEVOTION** ‚Äì Worship and thanksgiving (5 min)
2. **DECLARATION** ‚Äì Speak truth over yourself (5 min)
3. **DEPENDENCE** ‚Äì Pray for your needs (5 min)
4. **DEFENSE** ‚Äì Intercede for others (5 min)

**Why this works:**
- Prevents selfish "gimme" prayers
- Starts with God's character (Devotion) before our needs
- Balances personal prayer + intercession
- Teaches us to pray Scripture (Declaration)

### 3. Practice Together (30 min)
**Walk through each D live:**
- 5 min Devotion: "Who is God? What are we thankful for?"
- 5 min Declaration: "What truth from Scripture do we need today?"
- 5 min Dependence: "What do we need from God today?"
- 5 min Defense: "Who needs our prayers?"

### 4. Challenge + Closing (20 min)
**This week's challenge:**
- Pray 4D daily for 7 days (20 min total)
- Pair it with your 3D Journal (30 min total daily)
- Share one answered prayer in group chat
