### 2. Outreach Debrief (15 min)
**Ask the group:**
- "What did you experience during outreach?"
- "Where did you see God show up?"
- "What challenged you? What encouraged you?"
- "Any conversations or moments that felt significant?"
- "How did this grow your faith?"

**Leader's role:**
- Celebrate every story‚Äîbig or small
- Help them see God in moments they might overlook

**Transition:** "Many of you just shared fresh testimonies. Let's learn how to capture and share these stories powerfully."

### 3. Leader Testimony Modeling (10 min)
**Leader 1 shares first testimony (3-5 min):**
- Share naturally and compellingly
- After sharing, pause and debrief:
  - "What made that easy to follow?"
  - "What details stood out?"
  - "What did you learn about God?"

**Leader 2 shares second testimony (3-5 min):**
- Recent "God moment"
- Debrief what made it effective

### 4. Teaching: The STORY Framework (15 min)
**The 5 Parts:**
- **S - STRUGGLE (Before)** ‚Äì Where were you before God intervened?
- **T - TURNING POINT (The Moment)** ‚Äì When/how did God show up?
- **O - OUTCOME (After)** ‚Äì What changed as a result?
- **R - REFLECTION (What You Learned)** ‚Äì What did this teach you about God?
- **Y - YOUR INVITATION (The Call)** ‚Äì What hope can you offer others?

**What makes testimonies POWERFUL:**
‚úÖ Specific (not vague)
‚úÖ Concise (3-5 min max)
‚úÖ God-centered (He's the hero)
‚úÖ Honest (include the struggle)
‚úÖ Relatable (others see themselves in your story)

### 5. Testimony Workshop (35 min)
**Step 1: Pair Up (5 min)**
- Break into pairs
- Each person shares which testimony they want to focus on

**Step 2: Workshop in Pairs (20 min - 10 min each)**
- Person 1 shares testimony draft
- Person 2 gives feedback:
  - "What part was most compelling?"
  - "Where could you add specific details?"
  - "Is the STORY Framework clear?"
  - "Is God the hero?"
- Switch roles

**Step 3: Volunteer Shares (10 min)**
- 2 volunteers share refined testimony with whole group
- Group gives feedback and affirmation

### 6. Challenge + Commission (10 min)
**This week's challenge:**
1. Refine your testimony using tonight's feedback
2. Practice out loud
3. Share with someone outside this group
4. Add new "God moments" to your worksheet
5. Pray for opportunities to share
