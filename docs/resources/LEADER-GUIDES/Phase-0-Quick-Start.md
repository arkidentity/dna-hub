## ğŸš¨ COMMON PHASE 0 MISTAKES

### Mistake #1: Skipping the co-leader
**Why it's a problem:** You'll burn out, and no one will multiply.
**Solution:** Don't start without a co-leader. Wait if you need to.

### Mistake #2: Inviting too many people
**Why it's a problem:** DNA groups work best with 4 people (1 leader + 1 co-leader + 2 disciples).
**Solution:** If more than 2 people say yes, either start 2 groups or have them wait for the next group.

### Mistake #3: Not setting clear expectations
**Why it's a problem:** People drop out when reality doesn't match expectations.
**Solution:** Over-communicate the commitment level. Better to scare them off now than lose them at Month 6.

### Mistake #4: Rushing the invitation
**Why it's a problem:** You pick the wrong people or they say yes without understanding.
**Solution:** Follow the 6-week process. Don't skip the prayer and coffee conversations.

### Mistake #5: Starting without the DNA Group Agreement
**Why it's a problem:** No written commitment = easy to back out later.
**Solution:** Have EVERY disciple sign the agreement before Week 1.
