## ‚è±Ô∏è ACTIVITY STRUCTURE (2-3 HOURS)

### 1. Pre-Outreach Huddle (20 min)
**Before you go:**
- Cast vision: "We're representing Jesus today."
- Pray together:
  - For boldness and courage
  - For divine appointments
  - Against fear and spiritual opposition
- Assign roles: Pair people up (experienced with less experienced)
- Quick training: How to approach, how to share gospel, how to handle rejection

### 2. The Outreach (90 min)
**During the activity:**
- Leaders model first, then release disciples
- Stay in pairs for safety
- Look for Holy Spirit highlights
- Be bold but not pushy
- Pray when prompted

**Simple script:**
"Hey, we're from [church/group] and we're out praying for people today. Is there anything we can pray for you about?"

### 3. Debrief Immediately After (30 min)
- Share stories: "What happened?"
- Celebrate wins: "Who prayed for someone for the first time?"
- Process challenges: "What was hard?"
- Pray thanksgiving

### 4. Reminder for Next Week (5 min)
"Next week is Testimony Time. We'll debrief this experience fully AND workshop your testimonies. Make sure you've filled out the Testimony Development Worksheet I sent you‚Äîadd experiences from today!"
