### BEFORE DNA DISCIPLESHIP MANUAL

#### 1. Personal Dam Assessment
**When:** Before starting the DNA Discipleship Manual (optional but recommended)
**Who takes it:** Future DNA leaders
**Duration:** 15-20 minutes
**Purpose:** Identifies 7 potential blockages ("dams") to spiritual growth

**The 7 Dams:**
1. Unforgiveness
2. Unbelief
3. Pride
4. Fear
5. Shame
6. Idolatry
7. Religious Spirit

**What happens next:**
- Address any major blockages before starting discipleship training
- Use results to guide personal prayer and breakthrough work
- Not required, but helps you start with a clean slate

**Where to take it:** DNA Groups Dashboard or ARK Identity app

---

### AFTER DNA DISCIPLESHIP MANUAL (SESSION 6)

#### 2. DNA Readiness Quiz
**When:** After completing all 6 sessions of the DNA Discipleship Manual
**Who takes it:** Future DNA leaders preparing to enter Phase 0
**Duration:** 10-15 minutes
**Purpose:** Confirms you're ready to lead a DNA group

**Assessment Categories:**
1. **Spiritual Maturity** - Consistent devotional life, freedom from bondage
2. **Life Stability** - Capacity to commit for 12 months
3. **Leadership Readiness** - Natural spiritual conversations, initiative
4. **DNA Competencies** - Knowledge of phases, tools, and theology

**Passing criteria:**
- Score 70%+ overall
- No critical red flags (active addiction, major life crisis, theological confusion)

**What happens next:**
- If ready: Proceed to Phase 0 (Pre-Launch)
- If not ready: Address gaps, take quiz again in 4-6 weeks

**Where to take it:** DNA Groups Dashboard

---

### WEEK 1 OF DNA GROUP (PHASE 1)

#### 3. Life Assessment (Week 1 Baseline)
**When:** First official DNA group meeting (Week 1)
**Who takes it:** All disciples AND leaders
**Duration:** 20-30 minutes
**Purpose:** Establishes baseline measurement of spiritual health across 7 life categories

**The 7 Life Categories:**
1. **Relationship with God** - Prayer life, Bible engagement, worship
2. **Spiritual Freedom** - Freedom from sin patterns, shame, spiritual oppression
3. **Identity & Emotions** - Understanding identity in Christ, emotional health
4. **Relationships** - Quality of family, friends, church relationships
5. **Calling & Purpose** - Clarity on gifts, calling, kingdom contribution
6. **Lifestyle & Stewardship** - Time, finances, health, Sabbath rhythms
7. **Spiritual Fruit** - Evidence of love, joy, peace, patience, etc.

**Format:**
- 42 total questions (6 per category)
- Mix of 1-5 rating scale + open-ended reflections
- Completed online during the meeting (wifi required)

**What happens next:**
- Leaders review results in DNA Groups Dashboard
- Use insights to identify where disciples need focus
- Results saved for comparison at Week 12

**Where to take it:** Link sent from DNA Groups Dashboard

---

### WEEK 11 OF DNA GROUP (PHASE 1)

#### 4. Spiritual Gifts Assessment
**When:** Week 11 of the 90-Day Toolkit (Month 3)
**Who takes it:** All disciples
**Duration:** 15-20 minutes
**Purpose:** Discover spiritual gifts and God-given design for serving

**Assessment Categories:**

**TIER 1: HOW YOU SERVE** (Everyone has these)
- Romans 12 gifts: Teaching, Serving, Encouraging, Giving, Leading, Mercy, Prophecy

**TIER 2: SUPERNATURAL EMPOWERMENT** (Gifts you grow into)
- 1 Corinthians 12 gifts: Wisdom, Knowledge, Faith, Healing, Miracles, Prophecy, Discernment, Tongues, Interpretation

**TIER 3: LEADERSHIP CALLING** (Some, not all)
- Ephesians 4 five-fold: Apostle, Prophet, Evangelist, Shepherd, Teacher

**Format:**
- Questions about natural tendencies, desires, and experiences
- Measures both DESIRE (what you want) and EXPERIENCE (what you've done)

**What happens next:**
- Week 11 meeting includes impartation and activation of gifts
- Disciples practice using their gifts immediately
- Use results to help disciples find their role in the Body

**Where to take it:** DNA Groups Dashboard (sent 3-4 days before Week 11)

---

### WEEK 12 OF DNA GROUP (PHASE 1)

#### 5. Life Assessment (Week 12 Comparison)
**When:** Final meeting of the 90-Day Toolkit (Week 12)
**Who takes it:** All disciples AND leaders
**Duration:** 20-30 minutes
**Purpose:** Measure growth over 12 weeks by comparing to Week 1 baseline

**Same 42 questions as Week 1**

**What's different:**
- Dashboard auto-generates a **Week 1 vs Week 12 Comparison Report** for each disciple
- Shows percentage change in each of the 7 life categories
- Highlights specific questions with biggest growth
- Displays open-ended responses side-by-side

**What happens next:**
- Week 12 meeting dedicated to celebrating growth
- Identify areas still needing work
- Set goals for Phase 2 (Months 4-6)
- Comparison report saved for future reference

**Where to take it:** Link sent from DNA Groups Dashboard

## üí° IMPORTANT NOTES

### Assessment Best Practices

**1. Take assessments DURING meetings (not before)**
- Life Assessments (Week 1 and 12) are completed live as a group
- This creates shared experience and vulnerability
- Leaders can support anyone who gets stuck

**2. Be honest, not "spiritual"**
- Answer based on reality, not how you wish things were
- Assessments only help if you're truthful
- No one is grading you‚Äîthis is for YOUR growth

**3. Leaders take assessments too**
- Model vulnerability by completing alongside disciples
- Your results help you identify your own growth areas
- You're not above the process‚Äîyou're in it together

**4. Results are private**
- Only leaders see individual assessment results (via Dashboard)
- Disciples choose what to share with the group
- Create a safe, judgment-free environment

**5. Don't skip assessments**
- Each one serves a strategic purpose in the DNA journey
- Skipping assessments means missing key growth insights
- The system works best when followed sequentially

## ‚ùì FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

**Q: Do I have to take the Personal Dam Assessment?**
A: It's optional but highly recommended. Many leaders discover breakthrough by addressing "dams" before they start leading.

**Q: What if I "fail" the DNA Readiness Quiz?**
A: You can't fail‚Äîit's a diagnostic tool. Low scores simply mean "not yet." Address the gaps and retake it when ready.

**Q: Can disciples see each other's Life Assessment results?**
A: No. Results are private between the disciple and leaders. Disciples share voluntarily during Week 1 and 12 discussions.

**Q: What if someone scores low on Spiritual Gifts Assessment?**
A: There are no "low" scores‚Äîjust different designs. Some people score high in Tier 1 (serving), others in Tier 2 (supernatural). Both are valuable.

**Q: How accurate is the Week 1 vs Week 12 comparison?**
A: Very accurate IF disciples are honest on both assessments. The comparison shows real growth patterns over 12 weeks.

**Q: What if a disciple isn't ready to multiply after 12 months?**
A: That's okay\! The Multiplication Readiness Checklist helps you decide together whether to extend Phase 3 or proceed with multiplication.
